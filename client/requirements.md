## Packages
gsap | Advanced animations, timeline sequencing, and horizontal scroll with ScrollTrigger
lenis | Smooth scrolling required for the fluid, luxury feel requested
@studio-freight/react-lenis | React bindings for Lenis smooth scrolling
react-fast-marquee | Infinite seamless loop for the partner marquee
lucide-react | High-quality icons for services and UI
framer-motion | Declarative entry/exit animations and spring physics
react-hook-form | Form state management for the contact section
@hookform/resolvers | Zod validation integration for the form

## Notes
- Relying on GSAP for complex timeline and scroll-bound animations.
- Relying on @studio-freight/react-lenis for the global smooth scroll.
- The custom cursor will replace the default browser cursor - ensure no overflow issues.
- Included fallback/placeholder structure for Services and Case Studies if the database returns empty arrays initially, ensuring the frontend still looks stunning.
